About Us

Bayview Rehabilitation is a multidisciplinary rehabilitation clinic that serves North York, Thornhill, Richmond Hill and surrounding communities. The clinic has been in operation for over 25 years in its current location and is designated as a Community Physiotherapy Clinic (OHIP) by the Ministry of Health which will allow it to provide physiotherapy services to those who meet Ministry guided requirements. For those who do not qualify for this type of funding, we provide physiotherapy in addition to other rehabilitation services using various fee-for-service models.

Privacy Policy

Bayview Rehabilitation is committed to collecting and disclosing your personal information in a responsible manner. Privacy of this information is essential to our values and a requirement of the Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA). We will use and disclose your personal information to:

  • To provide rehabilitation services
  • To inform you of your treatment options
  • To collaborate with other members of your health care team
  • To ensure we are able to contact you about your appointments and the services you receive.
  • To bill a third-party payer for our services if you have been involved in a MVA and/or at the discretion of the director or manager.
  • To process credit card payments.
  • To collect unpaid accounts.
  • To comply with federal and provincial law. generally with the law.

If you have any questions regarding our privacy policy or wish to review the contents of your records with Bayview Rehabilitation, please make the request is writing to our Privacy Information Officer by email, fax or regular mail.
